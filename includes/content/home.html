Grampian Emerald CSC

30 years on the one road

In December 1984 three Celtic fans caught an early train from Aberdeen to get to Celtic Park so they could queue for tickets for the forthcoming game against Aberdeen at Pittodrie. Whilst in the queue the three fans, Paul Main, Gary Law and Elaine Marioni discussed the possibility of forming a supporters' club to make access to Celtic matches more convenient. The rest is history...

The Grampian Emerald CSC the largest CSC in the North-East of Scotland with in excess of 150 regulars and run buses to all home and most away games in Scotland. Our policy is simple: to provide regular affordable travel to watch the Bhoys in action and to bring together Celtic fans who live or work in the area in a friendly atmosphere.
If you are interesting in joining the club please contact us on 07881 454122.
