Bus Information

Below is the main Bus departure location in Aberdeen, which is at the Woolmanhill / Skene Street layby, behind HMT, on the B9119.

Please join the Grampian Emerald Forum* or use the Facebook page if you have any questions. Call or text the Grampian Emerald on 07881 454122 to book a seat on the bus (preference will be given to members & regulars).

This seasons bus fares are set as follows:

Member Adults: £20

Member Students: £15

Secondary School Pupils: £10

OAPs: £10

Primary School Pupils: Free

Non-Member Adults & Students: £25

The Supporters’ Bus also picks up at the following points, for matches going south:

Dyce, at the New Greentrees. The bus will leave Dyce 30 minutes before the normal leaving time from Woolmanhill. Dyce, Victoria Street next to the train station, if required. Stoneywood Rd, If required.

Bucksburn, Auchmill Rd (opposite Newton Terrace).

Great Northern Road junction with Greenmore Gardens (Woodside, at Barron Street). Berryden Rd, if required.

We will stopping at our main terminus Woolmanhill for 10 minutes before moving on to the usual pick-up points:

Torry, next to Halfords (Balnagask Rd), Wellington Rd.

Altens adjacent to Shell Filling Station (Redmoss Park), Wellington Rd.

Portlethen, at Roadside of Cookston on the A90.

Stonehaven, opposite BP Filling Station on Dunnottar Ave.

Stracathro, beside the mini-roundabout before Stracathro Service Area (on request).

Dundee, on Kingsway A90 at Mains Loan (lower bus price).

For matches going north, i.e. ICT & Ross County, other pick up points will be arranged.
